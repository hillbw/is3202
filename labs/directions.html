Directions to Chia Vet Headquarters

Chia Vet is located in beautiful downtown Portland, overlooking the majestic Mount Hood (which houses our secret bunker of all Chia seed strains).

Address

700 SW 5th Ave.
Portland, OR 97204

Driving Directions

Chia Vet heaquarters are easily located by plane, car, helicopter, foot or moped.

From the North

  1. Take I-5 South
  2. Exit at 300B for OR-99E
  3. Keep right at the fork and follow the signs to Morrisson St/City Center
  4. Merge onto the Morrison Bridge
  5. Keep right onto the ramp to Salem/I-T S
  6. Merge onto SW Naito Parkway
  7. Turn right at SW Morrison St.

From the South

  1. Take I-5 North
  2. Exit at 299B on the left
  3. Take exit 1A toward Naito Parkway
  4. Merge onto SW Harbor Dr.
  5. Turn right at SW Naito Parkway
  6. Turn left at SW Morrison St.

For Geocachers

For GPS enthusiasts we're located at 45.518777, -122.677564. Don't forget to sign the logbook inside the giant Chia Hippo in the waiting room.
